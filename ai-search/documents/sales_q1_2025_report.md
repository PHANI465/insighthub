# Sales Performance Report — Q1 2025

**Document Type:** Sales Report
**Period:** January 1 – March 31, 2025
**Published:** April 14, 2025
**Owner:** Revenue Operations
**Distribution:** Internal — All Staff

---

## 1. Executive Summary

InsightHub delivered a strong start to fiscal 2025. Q1 revenue of **$8.4 million** exceeded the target of $7.8 million by **7.7%**, supported by strong demand for Electronics and Home & Garden products, effective campaign execution, and improved conversion rates from new customer acquisition programmes. Order volume grew 9.4% quarter-over-quarter from Q4 2024.

## 5. Customer Acquisition and Retention

- **847 new customers** acquired in Q1 2025, a 31.7% increase versus Q1 2024.
- **Campaign Alpha** (email re-engagement for lapsed customers) contributed 18% of new customer activations.
- Customer retention rate: 84.3% (customers who placed ≥2 orders in trailing 12 months).
- Average customer lifetime value (LTV) for Q1 cohort: $1,247 (projected 24-month).
