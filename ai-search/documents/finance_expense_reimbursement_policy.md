# Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Document Type:** Finance Policy
**Version:** 4.1
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Owner:** Finance Operations
**Review Cycle:** Annual

---

## 1. Purpose

InsightHub reimburses employees for reasonable and necessary business expenses incurred while performing their duties. This policy defines eligible expenses, approval thresholds, spending limits, and the submission process.

## 2. Eligible Expense Categories

| Category | Examples | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Business meals | Working lunches, team dinners | Must have business purpose documented |
| Travel | Flights, hotels, ground transport, mileage | See Travel Policy for full details |
| Client entertainment | Customer dinners, events | Manager approval required in advance |
| Training and conferences | Registration fees, course materials | Must link to role development goals |
| Office supplies | Small tools, stationery | Under $100; pre-approved purchases preferred |
| Mobile phone | Business-use portion of personal plan | Up to $50/month; submit monthly |

## 3. Spending Limits

### 3.1 Meals

| Meal | Per Person Limit |
|---|---|
| Breakfast | $25 |
| Lunch | $40 |
| Dinner | $75 |
| Full-day working meals | $120 |

Alcohol may be expensed only for client entertainment and is capped at 20% of the meal total, not to exceed $30 per person.

### 3.2 Mileage

Personal vehicle mileage is reimbursed at the current **IRS standard mileage rate** ($0.67 per mile as of January 2025). Employees must log trips (date, destination, business purpose, miles) in Concur.

### 3.3 Corporate Card

Employees whose monthly reimbursable spend exceeds $500 on average will be issued a company Mastercard. Corporate card expenses still require receipts and manager approval in Concur. Misuse of the corporate card is grounds for immediate termination.

## 4. Receipt Requirements

- Receipts are required for **all individual expenses over $25**.
- Receipts must be itemised (not just a credit card slip). For meals, the full restaurant receipt showing items ordered is required.
- Digital receipts are acceptable. Photographs of paper receipts must be clear and legible.
- Receipts must be submitted within **30 days** of the expense date. Late submissions (31–90 days) require VP approval. Submissions after 90 days will not be reimbursed.

## 5. Approval Thresholds

| Expense Total (per claim) | Required Approver |
|---|---|
| Under $500 | Direct manager |
| $500 – $2,000 | Director or Senior Manager |
| $2,001 – $5,000 | VP |
| Over $5,000 | CFO |

Claims from managers are approved by their own manager (one level up).

## 6. Submission Process

1. Log the expense in **Concur Expense** within 30 days of the expense date.
2. Attach all receipts and enter the business purpose for each line.
3. Submit for manager approval in Concur.
4. Finance reviews and processes approved claims in the **bi-weekly payroll cycle** (claims submitted by Friday noon are processed 10 business days later).

## 7. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following will not be reimbursed:

- Personal entertainment (cinema, sports events without a client)
- Fines, penalties, or traffic violations
- Childcare, pet care, or personal grooming
- Upgrades (first class, premium hotels above the $250 cap) without advance VP approval
- Alcohol purchased outside of approved client entertainment
- Gifts to colleagues (unless authorised by HR for exceptional circumstances)
- Expenses incurred by family members accompanying the employee

## 8. International Expenses

Expenses incurred in foreign currencies are converted at the exchange rate on the date of the expense (Concur pulls live rates automatically). Employees do not need to convert manually. Per-diem rates for international travel are **$150/day** for meals and incidentals.

## 9. Disputed Reimbursements

If a reimbursement is declined, Finance will provide a written reason within 5 business days. Employees may appeal to the Finance Director within 10 business days.
