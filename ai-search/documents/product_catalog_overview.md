## 3. Category Details

### 3.1 Electronics

Our largest revenue category by value. Key sub-categories:
- Smart home devices (thermostats, security cameras, hubs): 21 SKUs
- Wearables (smartwatches, fitness trackers): 14 SKUs
- Computer peripherals (keyboards, mice, monitors, webcams): 23 SKUs
- Audio (earbuds, speakers, headphones): 20 SKUs

**Reorder level:** 150 units (fast-movers); 50 units (slow-movers)
**Lead time:** 21–35 days (supplier-dependent)

### 3.2 Clothing & Accessories

Broadest SKU count in the catalog with strong margin performance. Key sub-categories:
- Casual apparel (T-shirts, jeans, sweaters): 45 SKUs
- Footwear: 28 SKUs
- Bags and accessories: 22 SKUs
- Formal wear: 17 SKUs

**Seasonal:** 30% of SKUs rotate seasonally (spring/summer, autumn/winter). Product managers notify procurement 90 days before season change.

### 3.3 Home & Garden

Fastest-growing category (22.7% revenue growth FY 2024). Key sub-categories:
- Furniture (desks, chairs, shelving): 32 SKUs
- Garden tools and outdoor furniture: 28 SKUs
- Kitchen appliances and tools: 20 SKUs
- Bedding and soft furnishings: 15 SKUs

Shipping note: bulky items (>20 kg) attract a $15 oversized shipping surcharge passed to the customer.

### 3.4 Sports & Outdoors

Strong margin and growing customer segment (fitness-focused millennials and Gen Z). Key sub-categories:
- Running and trail gear: 22 SKUs
- Gym equipment (home use): 18 SKUs
- Cycling: 15 SKUs
- Water sports: 11 SKUs
- Camping and hiking: 5 SKUs

### 3.5 Health & Beauty

Highest margin category. Strictly regulated; all products must hold relevant safety certifications before listing. Key sub-categories:
- Skincare: 22 SKUs
- Haircare: 15 SKUs
- Supplements and vitamins: 14 SKUs
- Personal care devices: 10 SKUs

**Compliance:** Supplement claims must be reviewed by Legal before listing. Any health claims require substantiation documentation.

### 3.6 Books & Media

Declining volume but highest gross margin. Physical books only; eBooks and digital media not currently in scope. Sub-categories: business & professional (18 SKUs), fiction (16 SKUs), children's (14 SKUs).

### 3.7 Food & Beverages

Smallest category by margin. Short shelf-life products require careful stock management. Products must comply with FDA labelling requirements. Categories: gourmet foods (10 SKUs), beverages (7 SKUs), health snacks (6 SKUs).

### 3.8 Toys & Games

Smallest category by SKU count. All products must comply with ASTM F963 toy safety standards. Categories: board games (5 SKUs), educational toys (4 SKUs), outdoor play (3 SKUs).

## 4. Pricing Review Process

Pricing is reviewed **twice per year**:

- **Q1 Review:** January 15 – February 28. Effective April 1.
- **Q3 Review:** July 15 – August 31. Effective October 1.

Price changes require Product Manager sign-off and Finance Director approval for changes exceeding ±15% on any SKU.

## 5. New Product Launch Process

1. Product Manager submits a New Product Request in Jira with business case and competitor analysis.
2. Product Review Board convenes (meets bi-weekly) to approve or reject.
3. Supplier terms negotiated by Procurement (target: net-60 payment terms, minimum 35% GM).
4. IT lists the product in the catalog system (Shopify + ERP sync).
5. Marketing creates listing content (photos, descriptions, keywords).
6. Launch date confirmed with Operations for inventory readiness.

Average time from request to launch: **6–8 weeks** for established suppliers; **10–14 weeks** for new suppliers.

## 6. Discontinued Products

Products below the reorder threshold and with fewer than 5 unit sales in any 90-day period are flagged for review. Discontinuation requires Product Manager and Finance approval. Stock is cleared via promotional pricing before the SKU is deactivated.
