# Sales Performance Report — Q2 2025

**Document Type:** Sales Report
**Period:** April 1 – June 30, 2025
**Published:** July 14, 2025
**Owner:** Revenue Operations
**Distribution:** Internal — All Staff

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Q2 2025 continued InsightHub's strong momentum with revenue of **$9.3 million**, exceeding the $9.1 million target by **2.2%**. Order volumes hit a new quarterly record at 13,247. The Summer Social campaign launched in May was the highest-performing campaign of 2025 to date, contributing $341,000 in attributed revenue at an ROI of 312%. Customer satisfaction reached 4.3/5, an improvement of 0.1 points over Q1.

## 5. Loyalty Programme Launch

InsightHub launched its **RewardHub Loyalty Programme** on May 1, 2025. Early results:

- 4,823 customers enrolled in the first 8 weeks
- Enrolled customers placed an average of 2.4 orders per quarter vs 1.7 for non-enrolled
- Enrolled customers' average order value: $762 vs $688 (non-enrolled)
- Revenue uplift attributable to loyalty programme (Q2): approximately $340,000
