# Vendor Management Policy

**Document Type:** Operations
**Version:** 2.0
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Owner:** Operations & Procurement
**Review Cycle:** Annual

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy governs the selection, onboarding, performance management, and offboarding of vendors and suppliers at InsightHub. It ensures that our vendor relationships deliver value, meet security requirements, and operate under clear contractual terms.

## 2. Vendor Risk Classification

All vendors are classified into one of four risk tiers based on their criticality to operations and access to company data:

| Tier | Classification | Criteria | Examples |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Critical** | Single-source supplier; revenue-critical; access to Restricted data | Cloud providers, payment processors |
| 2 | **High** | Important but replaceable; access to Confidential data | Marketing platforms, CRM vendors |
| 3 | **Medium** | Non-critical; access to Internal data only | Office supplies, training providers |
| 4 | **Low** | No system access; easily replaceable | Couriers, facilities vendors |

Tier classification is assigned by Operations and reviewed annually by Finance and IT Security.

## 3. Vendor Selection Process

### 3.1 Sourcing Requirements

| Contract Value | Minimum Quotes Required |
|---|---|
| Under $5,000 | 1 quote (documented) |
| $5,000 – $25,000 | 2 written quotes |
| Over $25,000 | 3 competitive quotes or formal RFP |

### 3.2 Due Diligence

Before awarding a contract, Procurement must complete the following, scaled by risk tier:

**All tiers:**
- Vendor contact details, registration, and trading history
- Bank account verification for payment setup

**Tier 1 and 2 additionally:**
- Financial stability check (last 2 years' accounts or Dun & Bradstreet report)
- Information security assessment completed by IT Security
- References from at least 2 existing customers of similar scale
- Insurance certificates (general liability, professional indemnity)

**Tier 1 only additionally:**
- SOC 2 Type II or ISO 27001 report (within 12 months)
- Business continuity plan review
- Data Processing Agreement (DPA) signed before any data is shared

### 3.3 Approval Authority

| Contract Value | Approval Required |
|---|---|
| Under $10,000 | Department Manager |
| $10,000 – $50,000 | VP |
| $50,001 – $200,000 | CFO |
| Over $200,000 | CEO + Board notification |

## 4. Contract Standards

All vendor contracts must include:

- **Payment terms:** Net-30 as standard (net-60 for large contracts over $100K)
- **Liability cap:** Limited to 12 months of contract value
- **IP ownership:** All deliverables created for InsightHub are owned by InsightHub
- **Data handling:** Compliance with InsightHub's Data Processing Agreement requirements
- **Termination:** 30-day termination for convenience; immediate for material breach
- **Audit rights:** InsightHub may audit vendor compliance with contracted terms (annual)
- **SLA penalties:** Defined for Tier 1 and Tier 2 vendors

Legal must review all contracts above $10,000 before signature.

## 5. Vendor Performance Management

### 5.1 Review Frequency by Tier

| Tier | Review Frequency | Format |
|---|---|---|
| Critical (Tier 1) | Quarterly Business Review | Formal; exec attendance |
| High (Tier 2) | Bi-annual review | Structured meeting |
| Medium (Tier 3) | Annual review | Scorecard + email |
| Low (Tier 4) | As needed | Informal |

### 5.2 Scorecard Dimensions

Tier 1 and 2 vendors are scored on:
- Delivery performance (on-time, quality): 40%
- Commercial performance (pricing, invoicing accuracy): 25%
- Responsiveness (SLA compliance, communication): 20%
- Security and compliance posture: 15%

Vendors scoring below 70% for two consecutive reviews are placed on a Vendor Improvement Plan. Scores below 60% trigger a sourcing review.

## 6. Annual Renewal and Cancellation

All vendor contracts are flagged for review **90 days before expiry**. Procurement sends renewal intent notices by **November 1** for contracts expiring in the following year. Decisions to renew or tender must be made by **December 15** to allow procurement lead time.

## 7. Vendor Offboarding

When a vendor relationship ends:

1. IT Security revokes all system access within **24 hours** of contract termination.
2. The vendor must provide a **Data Return and Destruction Certificate** within 30 days confirming all InsightHub data has been returned or securely deleted.
3. Procurement closes the PO and confirms final payment in the ERP system.
4. The vendor record is marked as **Inactive** in the Vendor Register.

## 8. Vendor Register

Operations maintains a central Vendor Register (in Confluence) listing all active vendors, tier classification, contract value, renewal dates, and performance scores. The register is reviewed quarterly by Operations and annually by the CFO.
