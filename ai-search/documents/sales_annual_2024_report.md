# Annual Sales Report — Fiscal Year 2024

**Document Type:** Sales Report
**Period:** January 1 – December 31, 2024
**Published:** February 10, 2025
**Owner:** Revenue Operations
**Distribution:** Internal — All Staff

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Fiscal Year 2024 was InsightHub's strongest year on record. Total revenue reached **$31.2 million**, exceeding the $29.0 million annual target by **7.6%** and representing **18.3% year-over-year growth** from FY2023. The business crossed the 48,000 orders milestone, serving nearly 10,000 unique customers globally. Gross margin improved to 35.9%, reflecting better category mix, reduced fulfilment costs, and disciplined promotional spend.

## 7. Customer Cohort Analysis
