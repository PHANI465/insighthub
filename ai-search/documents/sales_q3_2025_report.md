# Sales Performance Report — Q3 2025

**Document Type:** Sales Report
**Period:** July 1 – September 30, 2025
**Published:** October 15, 2025
**Owner:** Revenue Operations
**Distribution:** Internal — All Staff

---

## 1. Executive Summary

Q3 2025 saw InsightHub achieve its third consecutive quarter of above-target performance. Revenue reached **$10.1 million** against a target of $9.8 million (+3.1%). Sustained strength in Electronics and meaningful acceleration in Home & Garden, which grew 18% quarter-over-quarter, drove the outperformance. The customer base crossed the **10,000 active customer** milestone during the quarter.

## 5. Customer Milestone: 10,000 Active Customers

InsightHub crossed 10,000 active customers (placed at least one order in the trailing 12 months) in August 2025. This milestone reflects:

- Consistent new customer acquisition averaging 960 per quarter in 2025
- Retention rate improvement to **85.4%** (from 82.1% in Q3 2024)
- Reduction in churn driven by the loyalty programme launched in May 2025

## 9. Q4 2025 Outlook

Q4 is the company's largest revenue quarter, driven by holiday shopping. The target is **$11.5 million**, a 13.9% increase on Q3 actuals. Key initiatives:

- **Holiday Blitz Campaign** launching November 1 (budget: $180,000)
- Expanded gift-card programme with B2B corporate sales push
- Increased customer support staffing: +12 temporary agents from November 15
- Stock pre-positioning for top 50 SKUs to ensure availability through December 31
