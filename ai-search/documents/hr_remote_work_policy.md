# Remote Work Policy

**Document Type:** HR Policy
**Version:** 2.3
**Effective Date:** February 1, 2025
**Owner:** People Operations
**Review Cycle:** Annual

---

## 1. Purpose

InsightHub supports flexible working arrangements that enable employees to do their best work while maintaining team cohesion, data security, and service quality. This policy defines eligibility, expectations, and requirements for remote and hybrid working.

## 2. Work Models

InsightHub operates three official work models:

| Model | Description |
|---|---|
| **Hybrid** | Minimum 2 days per week in the office; remainder remote |
| **Remote-First** | Primarily remote with quarterly in-person team gatherings |
| **On-Site Required** | Full-time office presence required for operational reasons |

The work model for each role is agreed during the hiring process and documented in the employment contract. Changes to the agreed model require VP approval.

## 3. Eligibility for Remote Work

Employees are eligible for hybrid or remote-first arrangements provided:

- They have completed their **90-day probationary period** successfully.
- Their role is assessed as suitable for remote delivery (manager and HR determine this).
- Their performance is rated **Meets Expectations (3) or above** in the most recent review.
- They have a suitable and secure home working environment (see Section 6).

Employees on a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) must work on-site for the duration of the PIP.

## 4. Core Hours

All remote and hybrid employees must be available and responsive during **core hours**:

- **Monday to Friday, 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM** in their primary time zone.
- Meetings may only be scheduled within core hours unless all participants agree otherwise.
- Outside core hours, employees are encouraged to work asynchronously and should not feel obligated to respond immediately.

## 5. In-Office Requirements for Hybrid Employees

Hybrid employees must attend the office on:

- A minimum of **2 agreed days per week** (confirmed with manager at the start of each quarter).
- All-hands meetings, team off-sites, and client meetings when required.
- Their first 90 days (new starters are expected in-office 4 days per week during onboarding).

Office attendance is tracked via the building access system. Consistent non-compliance will be escalated to HR.

## 6. Home Office Requirements

Employees working remotely must ensure their workspace meets the following standards:

- A dedicated, lockable workspace free from interruptions during working hours.
- Reliable broadband connection with minimum 25 Mbps download / 10 Mbps upload.
- Company-issued laptop (personal devices are not permitted for work without MDM enrollment).
- VPN must be active whenever accessing company systems from outside the office network.
- Screen privacy filters required when working in public or shared spaces.

## 7. Equipment Allowance

To support a productive home office, InsightHub provides:

- **$500 one-time equipment allowance** for employees confirmed as hybrid or remote-first after probation. Eligible purchases: monitor, keyboard, mouse, webcam, desk accessories. Receipts required; reimbursed via payroll.
- **$50/month internet reimbursement** added to monthly payroll (taxable benefit). Submit via Workday expense claim.

Allowances are clawed back on a pro-rata basis if the employee leaves within 12 months.

## 8. Security Obligations

Remote employees bear heightened responsibility for information security:

- **VPN is mandatory** at all times when accessing internal systems or company data.
- Laptop must be locked when unattended, even at home.
- Video calls must not be taken in public spaces where the screen or audio may be overheard.
- Company data must not be stored on personal devices or personal cloud storage.
- Any suspected security incident must be reported to IT Security within 1 hour of discovery.

## 9. Travel and Expense

Remote-first employees who travel to the office are reimbursed for reasonable travel costs in line with the Travel Policy. Hybrid employees' regular commute costs are not reimbursed.

## 10. Policy Review and Amendments

This policy will be reviewed annually or after any significant change in business needs or employment legislation. Employees will receive 30 days' notice of any material change.
