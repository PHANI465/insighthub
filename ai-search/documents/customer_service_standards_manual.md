# Customer Service Standards Manual

**Document Type:** Customer Service
**Version:** 3.1
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Owner:** Customer Experience
**Review Cycle:** Semi-Annual

---

## 1. Purpose

This manual defines InsightHub's customer service standards, service level agreements (SLAs), escalation procedures, and quality benchmarks. All customer-facing team members must read and acknowledge this document.

## 2. Service Level Agreements (SLAs)

| Channel | First Response SLA | Resolution SLA |
|---|---|---|
| Live Chat | 2 minutes | 15 minutes |
| Phone | 90 seconds (hold time) | During call or same day |
| Email | 4 business hours | 1 business day |
| Social Media (public) | 1 business hour | 4 business hours |
| Social Media (DM) | 2 business hours | 1 business day |

SLAs apply during business hours: **Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM Eastern**. Saturday support is 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM (email and chat only). No Sunday support currently.

## 3. Key Performance Targets

| Metric | Target | Critical Threshold |
|---|---|---|
| Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) | 4.2 / 5.0 or above | Below 3.8 triggers review |
| First Contact Resolution (FCR) | ≥ 75% | Below 65% triggers team review |
| Average Handle Time (AHT) | Under 7 minutes | Above 12 minutes triggers coaching |
| Escalation Rate | Under 8% | Above 12% triggers process review |
| SLA Compliance | ≥ 92% | Below 85% triggers staffing review |

## 4. Ticket Categories and Handling

### 4.1 Billing Inquiries
- Verify customer identity before discussing account details (ask for order number + email).
- Credit or refund up to $50: agent discretion.
- Credit or refund $51–$200: Senior Agent approval.
- Credit or refund over $200: Team Lead approval.

### 4.2 Technical Issues
- Collect: device type, browser/app version, steps to reproduce, screenshot.
- If unable to resolve in 10 minutes, escalate to the Technical Support queue.
- Known issues: check the Status Page (status.insighthub.com) before troubleshooting.

### 4.3 Shipping Issues
- Delayed orders (more than 2 business days past estimated delivery): proactively issue a 10% discount code.
- Lost orders (carrier confirms lost): initiate replacement after 3 business days from loss confirmation.
- Damaged on arrival: photograph evidence required; replacement or full refund processed within 24 hours.

### 4.4 Returns and Refunds
- **30-day no-questions-asked return policy** (items must be unused and in original packaging).
- **60-day return window for defective items** (manufacturer defect confirmed by Technical Support).
- Return label is generated by the agent in the CRM; customer ships at InsightHub's expense.
- Refunds are processed within **3–5 business days** of the returned item being received.

### 4.5 General Inquiries
- Product availability, sizing, features: agents use the Product Knowledge Base in Confluence.
- Catalogue questions outside the Knowledge Base: escalate to Product Support specialist.

## 5. Escalation Path

```
Customer
   ↓
Customer Service Agent (Tier 1)
   ↓ (unresolved after 10 min or customer dissatisfied)
Senior Agent (Tier 2)
   ↓ (billing over $200, policy exception, VIP customer)
Team Lead (Tier 3)
   ↓ (legal threat, media escalation, executive contact)
Customer Experience Manager
```

Escalation must be accompanied by a full case note in the CRM before handoff.

## 6. Tone and Communication Standards

All customer communications must be:
- **Empathetic** — acknowledge the customer's frustration before moving to solutions.
- **Clear** — use plain language; avoid jargon and internal terminology.
- **Proactive** — offer resolution options before the customer asks.
- **Concise** — email responses under 200 words; chat messages under 3 sentences.

Scripted openers are provided in the Knowledge Base. Agents should adapt scripts naturally; robotic responses are discouraged.

## 7. Quality Assurance

- **10%** of all interactions are audited monthly by the QA team.
- QA scoring uses a 100-point rubric: greeting (10), understanding (20), resolution (40), tone (20), documentation (10).
- Scores below 75 trigger a coaching session within 5 business days.
- Scores below 60 trigger a formal performance conversation.

## 8. Sensitive Customer Situations

- **Vulnerable customers** (distress, financial hardship): transfer to Senior Agent immediately; offer payment plans.
- **Threatening behaviour:** end interaction, document in CRM, alert Team Lead.
- **Media or legal threats:** do not engage; escalate to Customer Experience Manager immediately.
