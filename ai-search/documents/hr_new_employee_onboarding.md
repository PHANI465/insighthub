# New Employee Onboarding Guide

**Document Type:** HR Guide
**Version:** 4.0
**Effective Date:** January 2025
**Owner:** People Operations
**Applies To:** All new hires, full-time and part-time

---

## 1. Welcome

Joining InsightHub means you are now part of a team building the future of business analytics. This guide walks you through everything that happens during your first 90 days. Your onboarding buddy and People Operations team are here to support you every step of the way.

## 2. Pre-Start Checklist (Before Day 1)

Within 3 business days of signing your offer letter, HR will contact you to:

- Collect identity verification documents (passport or driver's licence + proof of right to work)
- Send laptop shipping details or arrange office collection
- Share Workday access to complete tax forms and direct deposit setup
- Connect you with your assigned onboarding buddy

You will receive a welcome email from IT with your company email address and initial password **2 business days before your start date**. Do not share this password.

## 3. Day 1 — Orientation

| Time | Activity |
|---|---|
| 9:00 AM | Welcome call with HR (virtual or in person) |
| 10:00 AM | IT onboarding: laptop setup, MFA enrollment, VPN, Slack |
| 11:30 AM | Building access badge (if in-office) |
| 12:00 PM | Lunch with your manager and team |
| 2:00 PM | Tool access provisioning: Jira, Confluence, GitHub (if applicable), Workday |
| 3:30 PM | Meet your onboarding buddy |
| 4:30 PM | Complete mandatory Day 1 training modules (security awareness, data handling) |

## 4. Week 1 Priorities

- Attend team stand-up and sprint planning (if applicable)
- Read your team's documentation in Confluence
- Schedule 1:1 introductions with key stakeholders (list provided by manager)
- Complete the remaining mandatory compliance training (Code of Conduct, GDPR awareness)
- Set up your development environment (engineering hires: follow the Engineering README)
- Confirm your bank and tax details are correct in Workday by end of Week 1

## 5. 30-Day Milestones

By the end of your first 30 days you should:

- Understand InsightHub's product, customers, and competitive landscape
- Know the team's current OKRs and how your role contributes
- Have completed all mandatory training (the compliance portal tracks completion)
- Have submitted your first piece of work for review or shipped a minor task
- Complete your 30-day check-in with your manager (informal conversation)

## 6. 60-Day Milestones

By day 60:

- Be operating with increasing independence on assigned tasks
- Have built relationships with your cross-functional partners
- Understand the team's tools, workflows, and decision-making processes
- Complete your 60-day check-in with your manager

## 7. 90-Day Milestones (Probation Completion)

By day 90:

- Your manager will complete the probation review form in Workday
- You should be contributing meaningfully to team goals
- Agree your first official OKRs for the remainder of the review year
- Your manager will confirm probation passed or initiate an extension if needed
- HR will send you the probation outcome letter within 5 business days

## 8. Mandatory Training (All Employees)

The following courses must be completed within your first 30 days. Access is via the Learning Portal in Workday:

1. Security Awareness Fundamentals (45 min)
2. Data Privacy and GDPR (30 min)
3. Code of Conduct Acknowledgement (15 min)
4. Anti-Bribery and Corruption (20 min)
5. Workplace Health and Safety (20 min)

Failure to complete these within 30 days will be flagged to your manager and HR.

## 9. Your Onboarding Buddy

Every new employee is assigned an onboarding buddy — a colleague (not your direct manager) who is available to answer informal questions. Buddy meetings should happen:

- 3×/week during Week 1
- 1–2×/week during Weeks 2–4
- As needed thereafter
