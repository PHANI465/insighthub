# Annual Leave Policy

**Document Type:** HR Policy
**Version:** 3.2
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**Owner:** People Operations
**Review Cycle:** Annual

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy sets out the annual leave entitlements, accrual rules, and request procedures for all full-time and part-time employees at InsightHub. It ensures that employees can plan rest and recovery while the business maintains adequate staffing levels.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all permanent employees. Contractors, consultants, and temporary staff are excluded and governed by their respective agreements.

## 3. Annual Leave Entitlement

### 3.1 Full-Time Employees

All full-time employees are entitled to **20 working days** (four weeks) of annual leave per calendar year.

| Years of Service | Annual Entitlement |
|---|---|
| 0–2 years | 20 days |
| 3–5 years | 23 days |
| 6–10 years | 25 days |
| 10+ years | 27 days |

### 3.2 Part-Time Employees

Part-time employees receive a pro-rated entitlement based on their contracted hours relative to a 40-hour full-time week.

### 3.3 Accrual Rate

Leave accrues at **1.67 days per completed calendar month** for employees on the standard 20-day entitlement. Accrual begins on the first day of employment.

## 4. Public Holidays

Employees are entitled to **10 federal public holidays** per year in addition to annual leave. Employees working in states with additional gazetted public holidays will receive those days automatically.

## 5. Sick Leave

Sick leave is administered separately from annual leave. All full-time employees receive **10 paid sick days per calendar year**, non-accruing and non-transferable.

## 6. Carry-Over Rules

- A maximum of **5 unused leave days** may be carried over into the following calendar year.
- Carried-over leave must be used by March 31 of the following year or it will lapse.
- Employees with business-critical roles may request an extended carry-over of up to 10 days; this requires VP approval.

## 7. Leave Request Procedure

1. Employees must submit leave requests via the HR portal (Workday) with a minimum of **3 business days' notice** for absences of 1–3 days.
2. For absences of 4 days or more, **10 business days' advance notice** is required.
3. Line manager approval is required before leave is confirmed.
4. For absences exceeding 10 consecutive days, HR must be notified in addition to the line manager.

## 8. Emergency and Compassionate Leave

In cases of family bereavement or personal emergency, employees may access up to **5 days of compassionate leave** per year. This is granted at management discretion and is separate from the annual leave entitlement.

## 9. Leave During Probation

Employees in their **90-day probationary period** may accrue but not take annual leave unless approved by HR. Emergency leave is available during probation at management discretion.

## 10. Leave Pay-Out on Termination

On termination of employment, any accrued but untaken annual leave will be paid out at the employee's base salary rate, subject to applicable tax withholdings. Leave in excess of the entitlement taken will be deducted from the final paycheck.

## 11. Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|---|---|
| Employee | Submit timely requests; plan leave to minimise team impact |
| Line Manager | Approve or decline requests within 2 business days |
| HR | Maintain leave records; resolve disputes; policy updates |
| Finance | Process pay-outs on termination |
